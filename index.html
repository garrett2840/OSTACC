You're Not Alone...

Whether for a friend, for a student, or for yourself, the first step to feeling better is communicating the issue to an experienced person with access to resources- you have come to the right place, and you are making the right choice. You are making it possible for healing to take place, and that is a tremendously generous act; it takes strength to be good to the self or to others when life gets hard. You are not, and are never, alone- people are here for you and they care what you have to say; please, type away...
